About

Fun Pizza is an Indonesian restaurant chain which was founded in 2021 in Sukarame, Bandar Lampung by Irfan Jesia Abyakta. It provides pizza and other Italian-American dishes, including pasta, side dishes and desserts.

So far, we have opened two branches in Bandar Lampung, it's located at Ryacudu Street Number 12 Sukarame and at Mall Boemi Kedaton.

Our Special Menu has three pizzas. It's the Veggie Supreme Pizza which topping is full of vegetables, Pepperoni Pizza with our best pepperoni, and our Meat Lovers Pizza with full meat on its topping.

We also have pasta, side dishes, and desserts. On the spotlight we have the Beef Spaghetti with our special sauce, Garlic Bread as our best side dish, and Almond Cake as our most selling dessert.
